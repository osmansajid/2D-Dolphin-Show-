Dolphin Show

1.Dolphin will keep swimming in the pool in a circle
2.CLouds keep moving in the sky
3.pressing 's' will make the dolphin stop after it comes in front of the pool
  another 's' pressing will make the dolphin move again
4.By mouse click the ball will move on the head of the dolphin and keep spinning
  and another mouse click will drop the ball on the ground
